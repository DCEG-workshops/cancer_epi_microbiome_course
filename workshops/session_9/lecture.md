## Lecture: Summary and Special Considerations in Epidemiology

### Learning Objectives
- Understand sample size calculations for microbiome studies
- Learn from cautionary tales in the field
- Understand participant considerations
- Evaluate microbiome intervention studies
- Understand within-person variation

### Lecture Materials
[Lecture slides will be posted here]

### Key Concepts
- Intraclass correlation coefficient (ICC) and sample size
- Available sample sizes in cancer cohorts
- TCGA and other cautionary examples
- Interdisciplinary collaboration considerations
- Participant willingness and compliance
- Microbiome intervention study designs
- Temporal stability and within-person variation

### Course Summary
This session brings together all concepts from the course and provides guidance on designing and conducting rigorous microbiome studies in cancer epidemiology.
