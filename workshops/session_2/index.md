### Topics Covered
- Oral and fecal collection methods
- Questionnaires at collection
- Long-term storage of oral and fecal samples
- Development/use of QC samples
- Batching of samples to send to the laboratory

### Session Overview

This session covers best practices for sample collection, storage, and quality control in microbiome studies. We will discuss methods for collecting oral and fecal samples, proper storage protocols, and the development and use of QC samples.
