# Human Microbiome – Topics & Recommended Readings

## Topics Covered

- Background concepts on the human microbiome including functions, body sites, components, and communities
- The Human Microbiome Project & Microbiome Quality Control Project
- *Helicobacter pylori* as the prototypical bacterial carcinogen and interventions
- Bacterial mechanisms of carcinogenesis
- Examples of DCEG-led disease association studies
- NHANES Oral Microbiome
- Future directions

---

## Practical Sessions

- Background concepts and human microbiome overview
- Epidemiologic methods and microbiome studies
- Examples of DCEG studies
