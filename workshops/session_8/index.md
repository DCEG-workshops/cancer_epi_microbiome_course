### Topics Covered
- Alternative sequencing techniques (e.g., RNAseq)
- Background reduction in low biomass samples
- Contamination evaluation
- Tissue microbiome challenges and solutions
- Case study: Sherlock Lung

### Session Overview

This session explores the unique challenges of studying tissue microbiomes using Sherlock Lung as a case study. We will discuss techniques for working with low biomass samples and addressing contamination concerns.
