## Lecture: Meta-analysis & Data Sharing

### Learning Objectives
- Understand meta-analysis approaches for microbiome studies
- Learn about different sequencing platforms and pipelines
- Understand reference database options
- Learn data sharing requirements and best practices
- Understand IRB and privacy considerations
