Dr. Abnet is Director of the Metabolic Epidemiology Branch and a leading expert in the etiology of esophageal and gastric cancer and in the study of the oral microbiome. His research focuses on understanding cancer risk factors in high-incidence populations, examining nutritional factors, genetics, and oral microbiome associations with upper gastrointestinal cancers across diverse geographic regions.
